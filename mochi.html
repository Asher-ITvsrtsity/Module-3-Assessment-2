Description:

Experience the delightful allure of Red Bean Mochi, a beloved Japanese confection that embodies both elegance and indulgence. These soft and chewy rice cakes are skillfully handcrafted, enveloping a luscious filling of sweet red bean paste. With every bite, you'll be enchanted by the delicate texture and the heavenly combination of subtly sweet mochi and the rich, earthy notes of the red bean filling. Treat yourself to this delectable treat, perfect for a moment of blissful indulgence.
